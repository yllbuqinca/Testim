Prizren

Prizren Fortress (Kalaja e Prizrenit): The fortress offers panoramic views of the city and the surrounding area. It's a great place to explore and learn about the history of the region. The Old Stone Bridge (Ura e Goricës): This medieval bridge over the Bistrica River is an iconic symbol of Prizren and provides a scenic backdrop for photography. Shadervan Square: This central square is surrounded by Ottoman-era architecture, and it's a popular spot for locals and visitors alike to gather, relax, and enjoy the surroundings. Sinan Pasha Mosque: One of the most important Ottoman mosques in Kosovo, the Sinan Pasha Mosque is an architectural gem located in the heart of Prizren. Prizren League House (Lidhja e Prizrenit): The house is a historical building where the League of Prizren was established in 1878, playing a key role in Kosovo's history. The Albanian League of Prizren Museum (Muzeu Lidhja Shqiptare e Prizrenit): Located in the League House, this museum provides information about the League of Prizren and its significance. Stone Bridge and Gorani Neighborhood: Explore the old stone bridge and the Gorani neighborhood, known for its narrow streets and traditional architecture. Saint George's Cathedral (Shën Gjergji): An Orthodox cathedral with a rich history and impressive architecture. Hamam of Iljaz Bey (Hamami i Iljaz Beut): An Ottoman-era hamam that has been preserved and is now used for cultural events. Gazi Mehmet Pasha Hammam: Another well-preserved Ottoman hamam in Prizren, offering a glimpse into the city's past. Prizren City Museum (Muzeu i Qytetit të Prizrenit): Housed in a beautiful building, this museum exhibits artifacts that showcase the history and culture of Prizren. The Old Bazaar (Çarshia e Vjetër): Stroll through the historic Old Bazaar, where you can find traditional crafts, local products, and a variety of cafes and restaurants.
