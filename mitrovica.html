Mitrovica

The Bridge: The main bridge over the Ibar River is a symbolic divide between
the northern and southern parts of the city. It has played a significant role in
the city's recent history.

Gazimestan Monument Located just outside of Mitrovica, this monument commemorates
the Battle of Kosovo, an important historical event in the region.

City Park (Parku i Qytetit): A green space where locals gather, especially during
weekends. It's a good place for a stroll or a picnic.

Mining and Metallurgy Institute Museum: This museum provides insight into the
mining and metallurgy history of the region, which has played a
significant role in the city's development.

Mitrovica Rock School: A cultural and educational project that uses music as a tool for
reconciliation. It's an interesting place to learn about
efforts to bridge divides through music.

Orthodox Church of St. Dimitrije: Located in the northern part of the city,
this Serbian Orthodox church is an important cultural and religious site
.
Mitrovica City Stadium: If you're interested in sports, you might want to check
out the local stadium for any events or games.

Mitrovica Museum: This museum provides information about the history and culture
of the city and its diverse population.
